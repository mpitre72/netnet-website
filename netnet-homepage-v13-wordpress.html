Drift & Flow: The Two States of Agency Work

Some weeks, everything clicks.
Other weeks feel off.

We call this 'drift and flow.' If you run an agency, you already know it.

Drift is the quiet slide: work outpacing your plan before anyone notices. Flow is when effort and plan stay in sync. Most agencies live somewhere in between and can't see exactly where. Net Net shows you.

Drift

Drift happens. It's part of the business. It's the quiet slide. Scope grows. The work takes more than anyone expected. By the time you notice, the job is already off track.

Flow

Effort and plan aligned. Your team delivers on pace. Problems surface early. You act before they compound.
